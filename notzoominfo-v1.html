Core Thesis
NotZoomInfo is not a product launch. It's a distribution loop disguised as a free tool. The mechanic:
  • Rehman posts "reply with a domain" on X
  • People reply publicly → visible engagement → more people reply
  • Public teaser (signal count + 2 headlines) → full report in DMs
  • DM includes permalink (notzoominfo.com/run/domain) → they share internally and externally
  • Every shared permalink is a Saber ad with an email capture
The product demonstrates itself. Every signal run is proof. Every permalink is a shareable case study.
